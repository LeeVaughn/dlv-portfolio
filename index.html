About Me

I am a Front-End Developer looking for opportunities to use and grow my knowledge of JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I use responsive design and mobile first principles to create functional and visually appealing web applications.

Before becoming a Developer, I spent over 20 years in hospitality management. My experience managing hotel and casino operations has given me a wide array of soft skills like critical thinking, time management, and team building.

These skills and others make me more than just the code I write. They make me a well-rounded person capable of contributing to the success of a team in a variety of ways.

When I am not writing simple yet elegant code, I enjoy spending time at the beach with my lovely wife, cheering on the best NHL expansion team ever (Go Golden Knights!), and enjoying a delicious pepperoni pizza.
